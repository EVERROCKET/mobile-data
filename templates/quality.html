Quality: About This Data Set

When I looked at the raw data I realized that it would be difficult to clean and bin the data so that it could be more easily visualized to tell a story. As the locations and activities were collected every few minutes, and they were across the whole month, I needed to deleted some of them to make the time gap between collected data larger. Since I wanted to explore the relationship between the time (frequency) and locations and the relationship between the time (in hours) and activities, the cleaned data has a relatively good quality. But some of them might not be accurate and some are also missing.

Is the data complete?

It seems to me that there is no significant amount of missing data. Every day, the amount of data is similar. Or, the missing data doesn’t affect much on my goals.

Is the data coherent?

It does make sense to me. In the locations data, the heat map I created matched perfectly my daily/weekly routine. I haven’t even thought about specifically the places I spent most of my time every day and week. When the map came out, I thought that is actually how I spend my time.

Is the data correct?

Initially, I didn’t recogize some of the heated places on the map, but most of them made sense to me. For the activities, the data recognized I was running in some time. They were at night and morning. However, I didn’t run in these time. I guessed it might be me just getting on the buses? For other parts, the data were correct from my expectations.
